About Our Team

Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying)

Maryam Soltanzadeh, (She, Her)

Psychotherapist specializing in children with diverse needs and families. Understanding the unique stages they undergo is crucial. Empowering and educating parents is central; belief in our children's greatness is key. Our children can be great, and they will be great, they just need someone to believe in them.

Child Facilitator - MA Educational Psychology

Masoud Ahmadi, (He, His)

Fascinated by human behavior, I pursued psychology, focusing on prevention in mental health. Specializing in educational psychology, I explored human development and nature's role in therapy. Since 2016, I've led nature-based programs. In 2019, I delved into psychoanalysis. Now, as a facilitator and psychotherapist, I help people navigate their inner worlds.

MA Clinical Psychology (UT), MA Gender and Social Justice Studies (McMaster)

Mina Esfandiari, (She, They)

I am a trauma-informed Psychoanalytic Psychotherapist with nearly a decade of experience. I specialize in anxiety, depression, trauma, PTSD, C-PTSD, and issues like sex, immigration, grief, pregnancy, abortion, and adoption. I believe our sufferings stem from personal, intergenerational, and socio-political traumas, and I offer culturally-sensitive, gender-affirming therapy.

OurStory

In the wide land where squirrels play on pine trees, there is a place known as "Roots and Tales." This place protects all stories for those who want to discover their roots.

Roots express the nature of our therapeutic approach. Roots are elements that are beyond our control. Parents, genetic information, childhood years, and everything we haven't chosen but has influenced our personality. We have realized that without understanding all these elements, we cannot achieve a proper understanding of personality.

Continuing on, every tale is a unique story shaped and experienced by the individual. Each person holds an untold story with moments of both sadness and joy. Beyond simply wanting to be good listeners, our goal is to see the world from your eyes and actively become a part of your special story.
